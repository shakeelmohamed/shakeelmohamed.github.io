Salgirah International Festival Identity

Branding, Environmental, Motion

Client: The Ismaili community

Tools: Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Photoshop, Rhino

Goal: Update a unified festival identity for the global Ismaili community.

Salgirah is a religious festival observed by Ismaili Muslims marking the birthday of their spiritual leader. His Highness the Aga Khan V, a descendant of Prophet Muhammad, was appointed the role of 15 million Ismailis worldwide after his father passed away earlier that year. October 12, 2025 marked his first birthday, or Salgirah.


The identity launched to great success with over 1 million impressions on social media across 21 countries in 10 multiple languages. This was my fifth year working on the identity, it’s always a joy to see how the system is used around the world from signage to cake decorations by all age groups. This year was particularly exciting to see communal artwork created using unexpected materials, see videos below.

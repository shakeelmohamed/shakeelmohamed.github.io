Planetary Society Rebrand

Media: Branding, creative coding, motion graphics, strategy, type design

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, After Effects, Glyphs, three.js, Houdini, Keynote, Figma

Goal: Bring philosophical and meditative ideas into the visual language of space exploration

Recognition: ArtCenter student gallery

The Planetary Society is a non-profit organization focused on advocacy for space exploration and based in Pasadena, California. Bill Nye is currently their CEO. Though the Planetary Society focused on space enthusiasts in general, I wanted to expand that a bit. By exploring the profound human experience of our relationship to the massive scale of the cosmos. 2025 marks 45 years since the Planetary Society was founded. I designed promotional posters for a 45th anniversary retreat on Catalina Island for members and donors. The 45th anniversary identity takes on a distinct color palette and typographic voice inspired by the natural beauty of Catalina Island.

Planetary Society Sans is a variable weight font I designed to be the typographic voice for their 45th anniversary. The modular typeface is inspired by planetary orbits, and can be used to create dynamic compositions when used at large scale.

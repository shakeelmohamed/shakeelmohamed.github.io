Last Updated: February 03, 2025

The true power of graphic designers is realized when we apply our thinking to more than just visual problems. Our industry’s obsession with “the best” is suffocating our creativity through a linear process. It’s time for a new way to understand creative projects, one which allows for room to meander. Design is a labyrinth, not a maze, a mindful creative exploration through skills, projects, collaborators, and insights.

I recently gave a talk titled “Deconstructing ‘Best’: Redefining Success for Designers” at a conference hosted by AIGA Los Angeles in November 2024.

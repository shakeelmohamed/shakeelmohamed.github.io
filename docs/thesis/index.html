Last Updated: December 28, 2024

This page will get an overhaul in the coming weeks, until then follow along on Instagram @applieddesigner.

The true power of graphic designers is realized when we apply our thinking to more than just visual problems.

Our industry’s obsession with “the best” is suffocating our creativity through a linear process.

It’s time for a new way to understand creative projects, one which allows for room to meander.

Design is a labyrinth, not a maze, a mindful creative exploration through skills, projects, collaborators, and insights.

I recently gave a talk titled “Deconstructing ‘Best’: Redefining Success for Designers” at a conference hosted by AIGA Los Angeles.
